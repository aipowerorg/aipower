# Create / Edit Bot

You can deploy chatbots either as a shortcode or as a widget. 

Follow the steps below to create or edit a bot.

## Creating a Shortcode

- Go to the **Dashboard - Chatbot** tab.
- Click the **Create New Bot** button.

<img src={CreateNewBot} />

- A shortcode will be automatically generated at the top of the page.

<img src={CreateNewShortcode} />

- Copy the shortcode and insert it into your desired pages.

<img src={AddNewShortcode} />

## Creating a Widget

### Site-wide Widget

- Go to the **Dashboard - Chatbot** tab.
- You will see a **Site-wide Widget** option with a toggle next to the **Edit** button.
- Toggle the switch to activate or deactivate the widget across the entire site.

<img src={EnableSiteWideWidget} />

### Page-Specific Widget

To create a widget for specific pages:

- Turn off the **Site-Wide Widget**.
- Click the **Create New Bot** button.

<img src={CreateNewBot} />

- Under **General Settings**, find the **Bot Type** radio group.
- Change the bot type from **Shortcode** to **Widget**.

<img src={SelectingBotType} />

- Three additional fields will appear:
  1. **Position**: Choose whether the widget will appear on the bottom left or right of the screen.
  2. **Page / Post ID**: Enter the IDs of the pages or posts where you want the widget to appear, separated by commas.
  3. **Delay**: Set the delay time in seconds (e.g., enter "5" to automatically show the widget after 5 seconds).

## Editing an Existing Bot

- Go to the **Dashboard - Chatbot** tab.
- Find the bot you want to edit and click the **Edit** button to modify its settings.

<img src={EditingBot} />

## Other Actions

In the **Dashboard - Chatbot** tab, you will find additional actions for managing your chatbots.

<img src={BotActions} />

- Above the bot list, there are three options accessible by clicking the **Settings** icon on the right:
  1. **Delete All Bots**: Remove all chatbots in one action.
  2. **Export All Bots**: Download a file containing all your chatbot configurations.
  3. **Import**: Upload a file to import chatbot configurations.

In the **Actions** column of the bot table, you have the following options for each bot:

<img src={SinlgeBotActions} />

- **Eye Icon**: Preview the chatbot.
- **Trash Icon**: Delete that specific bot.
- **Duplicate Icon**: Clone the bot, creating a copy with the same settings.
- **Export Icon**: Export that specific bot's configuration.
